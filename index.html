Sathish Chandra Pichika is currently employed as Statistsician in University of Toronto. Prior to joining University of Toronto, he worked as Research Assistant in McMaster. He completed his M.Sc in December’11 at McMaster University, Canada, where he was supervised by Dr. Joseph Beyene. He received his B.Sc and M.Sc degree from Loyola College, Chennai, India in 2009. His current research focus is on Probability, structural change-point modeling, data science, repeated measure data analysis.
