Sathish Chandra Pichika is pursuing his Ph.D. in Statistics at the University of Windsor working under the advisor Dr. Severein Nkurunziza. Also, he currently employed as Statistical Analyst in University of Toronto working with Dr. David JA Jenkins. In the past, he worked as Research Assistant in McMaster University, where he completed his M.Sc in December’11 at McMaster University, Canada, where he was supervised by Dr. Joseph Beyene. He received his B.Sc and M.Sc degree from Loyola College, Chennai, India in 2007 and 2009. His current research focus is on Probability, structural change-point modeling, data science, repeated measure data analysis.
